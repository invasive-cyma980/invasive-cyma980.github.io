⚙️ Getting Started

The first time you open the program, the system asks for your audio settings. Select your audio interface in the settings menu. If you do not have a separate interface, choose the ASIO4ALL driver to ensure low latency. Low latency helps you hear sounds instantly when you play your keyboard or controller.

🎧 Workflow Optimization

Use the step sequencer to build drum patterns. Drag sounds from the browser pane into the project playlist to arrange them. Open the piano roll to edit melodies. You can add plugins to the mixer to change the sound of your audio. Use the master channel to control the volume of your final project.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

If you experience lag, go to the audio settings and increase the buffer size. This reduces the load on your computer processor. If you cannot hear sound, check your master output routing in the mixer to ensure it connects correctly to your speakers or headphones.
